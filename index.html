Seattle Minds & Machines

Welcome! Seattle Minds & Machines is a talk series focusing on neuroscience and AI based in Seattle. Drawing inspiration from the RLDM conference, we aim to foster interaction and cross-pollination between the related fields of neuroscience, cognitive science, and AI. We organize on a roughly monthly cadence and aim to have one speaker from neuro/cognitive science and one speaker from machine learning / AI. Our sessions are highly interactive and we leave lots of time for discussion both during and after the talks.

About the organizers

We are a group of neuroscience and machine learning researchers based in Seattle, WA. Our affiliations include the Allen Insitute for Neuroscience, the University of Washington, Intel, and Google DeepMind.
